How our classes are different

At SXYFIT Studio ™, every class is part of a bigger picture—a proven, strategic training system designed to deliver real, lasting transformation. We don’t throw random workouts on the schedule and call it a day.
Every session is carefully crafted around our 4-phase signature method: Ignite, Burn, Sculpt, and Shred.

These aren’t just cute names—they’re part of a deliberate progression that builds strength, burns fat, shapes your body, and unlocks next-level fitness. Whether you’re training in-studio or following our programs online, your body is always moving forward—not just working out, but transforming with purpose.

Here’s how it works:

  • IGNITE: is all about firing up your metabolism, building a strong foundation, and activating the key muscle groups that drive results. Expect strength-focused sessions with controlled tempo and form-focused movement to prep your body for what’s coming.
  • BURN: turns up the intensity. These classes are designed to torch fat, improve endurance, and build metabolic power. You’ll see more supersets, circuits, and conditioning-style formats that get your heart pumping while still building lean muscle.
  • SCULPT: focuses on shaping, refining, and developing your physique. Think targeted strength work, hypertrophy training, and volume-based sessions that bring the muscle tone and definition.
  • SHRED: is your high-impact finish. These classes blend everything you’ve built—strength, endurance, and conditioning—for a complete metabolic challenge. You’ll feel fitter, leaner, tighter, and stronger than ever.

Each week, the class schedule is structured so you get the perfect mix of Ignite, Burn, Sculpt, and Shred principles across different class types. Whether it’s strength, conditioning, boxing, or mat pilates—every session is coded with the phase it belongs to, so you know exactly how it fits into your transformation.

No more guesswork. No more spinning your wheels. You’ll train with purpose. Move with intention. And see results faster—because your entire week is programmed for progress.
