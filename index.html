What is s30

S30 is for everyone, whether you're an advanced trainer or a beginner, with scalable workouts to fit your needs in a fully-immersive environment.

Our 45-minute classes feature lowlights, loud bass, and high BPM music with weights for strength and muscle building, conditioning for fitness and endurance and hybrid sessions for overall strength and cardiovascular improvement, all tailored by experienced trainers to make you feel the best version of yourself.
